Questions

Common questions.

How do I connect to my server?

After setup, you'll get a subdomain like yourname.chunkhost.com. Open Minecraft, go to Multiplayer → Add Server, and paste it in. No port numbers needed.

Can I install plugins and mods?

Yes. Your Pterodactyl panel has a built-in file manager. Drop your plugins into the /plugins folder (for Paper/Spigot) or your mods into /mods (for Forge/Fabric) and restart the server.

Can I upload my existing world?

Absolutely. Use the file manager in your panel to upload your world folder, then update the level-name setting in server.properties to match. We can help you via Discord if you get stuck.

What happens if I need more RAM?

Upgrades are fast and painless. Contact us on Discord or via your billing portal and we'll upgrade your plan — usually with no downtime.

Is there a contract or minimum commitment?

No contract. Monthly billing only. Cancel any time from your billing portal and you won't be charged again.

What server types do you support?

Paper, PaperMC, Purpur, Vanilla, Spigot, Forge, Fabric, BungeeCord, Velocity, and more. If you need a specific version or type, ask us on Discord.

Where are the servers located?

Currently in Eastern NA. This is ideal for players in North America, and decent for Europe. We'll be adding more locations as we grow.
